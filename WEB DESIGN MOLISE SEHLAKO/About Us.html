Our History

MoliseAdvertTravelSolutions was founded in 2010 in the heart of Maseru, Lesotho, with a vision to promote tourism in this beautiful country. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the most trusted travel solution providers in Lesotho, offering a wide range of services tailored to meet the unique needs of both local and international tourists.

Aims and Objectives

1).To promote eco-friendly tourism in Lesotho.
2).To provide memorable, personalized travel experiences.
3).To showcase the cultural and historical richness of Lesotho.
4).To offer top-notch customer service to all our clients.
